How to make a Proport Pot of Tea

～ Materials ～

Kettle

Tea Pot & Cups

Spoon

Water

Tea Leaves (of choise)

sweeter optional

～ Instructions ～

Set 1 :

Bring Kettle to Boil

Large bubbles will be rising continuously from the bottom of the kettle to the surface, & steem will be coming out ～ 100°C or 212°F

Set 2:

Rinse Teapot & Cups

Rinse in warm water, make sure to get all the corner. Worming up your china gentally, can pervent fractions, also this help to keep your tea warmer longer.

Set 3:

Pour Boiling Water into Tea Pot

Pour the boiled water into the tea pot, ensuring you cover all the tea leaves.

Set 4:

Add Chosen Tea Leaves

Measure out the appropriate amount of tea leaves based on your teapot's size.

Set 5:

Let Steep

Let the tea leaves steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on the desired strength.

Set 6:

Poor and Enjoy!

Add sweeter if you would like.
